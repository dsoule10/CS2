Typography Reference Sheet!

Helpful reminders of some of the basic functions you can use for font-centric properties in CSS! This chart is not 100% comprehensive, but is thorough enough to keep your text lookin' stylish!

Compiled by Mr. David

Property Values Description
font-family font title Select a font in which your text will be displayed!
font-size px (pixels) Sets the size of your text in pixels.
font-weight 100-900, bold, lighter, bolder Sets the boldness(how thick or thin) your font will appear.
text-align left, right, center Controls the horizontal placement of your text.
text-transform uppercase, lowercase Sets your text to appear in all caps or not.
line-height px (pixels) or multiplier (no unit, just number) Determines how much vertical space will be placed between each line of your text.
letter-spacing px (pixels) Determines how much horizontal space will be placed in between each character.
word-spacing px (pixels) Determines how much horizontal space will be placed in between each word.
